How Same-Day Service Works

Same-day windscreen replacement means you call us in the morning and your new windscreen is fitted before the end of the day. Here's how we make it happen:

  • You call 0800 757-985 with your vehicle details and location
  • We check our stock — with 40,000+ pieces, we usually have your glass
  • We dispatch a mobile technician to your location across Auckland
  • Replacement takes 1–2 hours depending on vehicle complexity
  • You're back on the road with a lifetime-warrantied installation

Our technicians carry all necessary tools, adhesives, and mouldings. For most vehicles, the entire process from your call to completion can be under 4 hours.

What Makes Same-Day Service Possible

Two things make our same-day service possible that many competitors can't match:

  • Massive stock holding: Our Panmure warehouse carries one of New Zealand's largest auto glass inventories. Most auto glass shops need to order glass in — sometimes taking days or weeks. We already have it.
  • Fully mobile service: Our technicians come to you with everything needed. No need to drop your car off and arrange alternative transport. We work at your home, workplace, or any convenient location across Greater Auckland.

This combination of stock + mobility is what allows us to offer genuine same-day service, not just "we'll try."

Which Vehicles Qualify for Same-Day Service

Same-day service is available for the vast majority of vehicles on NZ roads. Here's a general guide:

  • Toyota, Nissan, Mazda, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, Ford, Holden, Mitsubishi — almost always same-day
  • Subaru, Suzuki, Volkswagen, Isuzu — usually same-day
  • BMW, Audi, Mercedes-Benz — often same-day, occasionally next-day for rare models
  • Commercial vehicles, vans, utes — typically same-day for common models

The key factor is whether we have your specific glass in stock. With 40,000+ pieces, the answer is usually yes. For very rare or newly released vehicles, next-day service may be needed.

ADAS Vehicles and Same-Day Service

Vehicles with ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems) require special consideration:

  • The glass replacement itself is same-day — we stock ADAS-compatible windscreens
  • Camera recalibration may be needed after replacement — we advise on this
  • Static recalibration (done in a workshop) may need separate booking
  • Dynamic recalibration (driving-based) can often be done immediately after fitting
  • Insurance typically covers recalibration as part of the claim

We only fit OEM-equivalent ADAS-compatible glass and ensure camera housings are positioned correctly. Even for ADAS vehicles, you'll rarely wait more than a day for the full job.

What to Expect on the Day

When our technician arrives, here's what happens:

  • Inspection: We confirm the damage and check for any additional issues
  • Preparation: The area around your windscreen is protected and prepared
  • Removal: Old glass is carefully removed without damaging paint or trim
  • Installation: New glass is fitted with OEM-spec adhesive and mouldings
  • Sensor check: ADAS cameras and rain sensors are positioned correctly
  • Clean-up: We leave your vehicle clean and ready to drive

We recommend waiting at least one hour after installation before driving, and 24 hours for full adhesive strength. Your technician will give specific guidance.
