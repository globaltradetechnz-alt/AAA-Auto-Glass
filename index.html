Common Questions

Windscreen Replacement Auckland — FAQs

Answers to the questions we hear most before booking windscreen replacement in Auckland.

For insured vehicles, windscreen replacement in Auckland is typically nil excess — you pay nothing. If you have a windscreen excess, that is all you pay (usually $100-$500 depending on your policy). For private jobs, windscreen replacement costs in Auckland start from around $650-$850 for standard vehicles and $800-$1,500+ for ADAS-equipped European models like BMW, Mercedes, Tesla, and Range Rover. Call us on 0800 757-985 for an exact quote.

We offer same-day windscreen replacement across Greater Auckland in most cases. Call us on 0800 757-985 before 2pm and we will get a technician to you as fast as possible — often within hours. With 40,000+ pieces of glass in stock at our Panmure warehouse, we rarely need to order parts in.

Yes — we are fully mobile. Our technicians come to your home, workplace, or any location across Greater Auckland. We cover the North Shore, East Auckland, South Auckland, West Auckland, Central Auckland, and Rodney. No need to drive to us.

We work with 36+ NZ insurance providers. We direct bill your insurance company and handle all paperwork. If nil excess there is nothing for you to pay. If you have a windscreen excess, that is all you have to pay. Call us and we will help you through the process.

We specialise in ADAS-equipped vehicles. We ensure all camera housings and sensors are correctly positioned after fitting and advise on calibration requirements. We only fit windscreens compatible with your camera and sensor systems.

Yes — they are our specialties. We carry OEM-quality glass for Tesla Model 3 and Y, and all Range Rover models including full HUD and sensor compatibility. Tesla owners travel from as far as Tauranga and Whangarei to use our service.

We use OEM-equivalent glass meeting or exceeding original factory specifications. For ADAS vehicles, we only fit windscreens compatible with your camera and sensor systems.

We recommend waiting at least one hour after installation. For full structural strength, 24 hours is ideal. We will give you specific guidance based on your vehicle.
