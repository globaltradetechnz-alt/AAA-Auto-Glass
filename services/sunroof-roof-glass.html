Sunroof & Roof Glass Replacement

A cracked or leaking sunroof is not just an eyesore — it can cause water damage to your vehicle's interior, create wind noise, and in extreme cases compromise the roof's structural integrity. At AAA Auto Glass, we replace sunroof and roof glass for all vehicle makes and models across Auckland.

Standard Sunroof Panels

Standard pop-up or sliding sunroof panels are the most common type we replace. We carry glass for popular vehicles and can source panels for rarer models. We ensure the new panel is sealed correctly and moves smoothly in its track.

Panoramic Roof Systems

Many modern SUVs and luxury vehicles have full-length panoramic roof systems. These are complex assemblies with multiple glass panels, motors, and drainage channels. We replace the glass panels and inspect the drainage system to prevent future leaks. Common vehicles with panoramic roofs include Range Rover, BMW X5, Audi Q5, and Mazda CX-9.

Tesla Roof Glass

Tesla Model 3 and Model Y have full glass roofs with no traditional sunroof — the entire roof is a single glass panel. If this glass is cracked or damaged, it needs specialist replacement. We carry OEM-quality Tesla roof glass and have the expertise to fit it correctly. Tesla owners travel from across the North Island to use our service.

What Causes Sunroof Damage?

  • Stone chips and road debris — especially on motorway driving at speed
  • Hail damage — Auckland's summer storms can crack sunroof glass
  • Tree branches — parking under trees or low-hanging branches
  • Structural stress — temperature changes can cause existing chips to spread
  • Seal failure — old or degraded seals can let water in, causing delamination

Mobile vs Workshop Fitting

Some sunroof replacements can be done mobile at your home or workplace, but larger panoramic roof panels may need to be fitted in a controlled environment. We will advise you when you book whether mobile fitting is suitable for your vehicle. Either way, we work around your schedule.

Insurance Coverage for Sunroof Glass

Sunroof and roof glass replacement is usually covered under comprehensive insurance policies. We handle the claim for you and invoice your insurer directly. Call us on 0800 757-985 with your policy details.
