Evoque Windscreen & Glass — What We Fit

The Evoque brought Range Rover style to the compact SUV class, and Auckland has plenty — the L551 first generation (2011–2018) and the current shape (2019 onward). It's one of our two specialist marques, so the tech in these screens doesn't faze us.

Evoque windscreens are option-sensitive: many have the heated fine-wire screen, most have rain/light sensors, later cars add the ADAS camera and some HUD. Tell us your registration and we match the exact specification — heated, camera, HUD, sensor pack — before we arrive. Evoque door glass, panoramic roof panels and tailgate glass are stocked or quickly sourced.

Related services: windscreen replacement, side & rear glass and ADAS windscreen work — all mobile, all across Auckland.
